Your preferred aircraft charter company to attend the 2026 FIFA Soccer World Cup in Mexico, USA and Canada.

Since the 2014 Brazil World Cup, the 2018 Russia World Cup and the 2022 Qatar World Cup, we are flying soccer fans and national teams to this magnificent event. In 2026, we will be flying fans and soccer teams from all over the world, to the following 16 cities in Mexico, Canada and the USA to attend the games.

🇲🇽 Mexico: Mexico City, Guadalajara, Monterrey.

🇨🇦 Canada: Toronto, Vancouver.

🇺🇸 USA: Seattle, Boston, Philadelphia, San Francisco, Houston, Kansas City, Dallas, New York/New Jersey, Los Angeles, Atlanta, Miami.

The 2026 FIFA Soccer World Cup will take place from June 11, 2026 through July 19, 2026, where 48 national soccer teams from the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Asia, Africa and Australia will dispute the World Soccer Cup.
We have A320s, B737s, Airbus 330s-350s, Boeing 777s-787s aircraft carrying 100-300 pax, as well as private jets, turboprops, helicopters and limousines.
